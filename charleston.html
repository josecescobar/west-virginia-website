Key Attractions

Main Attractions in Charleston, WV
Attraction Description
West Virginia State Capitol The iconic domed building housing the offices of the Governor and the West Virginia Legislature.
Clay Center for the Arts & Sciences A center that offers live performances, interactive science exhibits, and an art gallery.
Capitol Market An outdoor market offering fresh produce, flowers, and local specialties.
Kanawha State Forest A large state park with hiking, biking, and camping opportunities.
Charleston Coliseum & Convention Center The premier venue for sports, concerts, and other large events in the city.
Live on the Levee A summer concert series held along the Kanawha River, featuring free live music performances.
